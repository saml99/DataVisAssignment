Waste Collection in Australia



The line chart to the left shows the total number of waste items colected at each type of site each year. The buttons cna be used to show multiple site types on the graph and compare each of the site types for a given year or over the entire period of time. When the mouse is hovering over the points on the line chart the totals for that year will be displayed.

The stacked bar chart to the left shows the total waste found at each type of site for all years. The mouse can also be hovered over the individual colours in the bar to show the exact number of waste found at that site for the specific year.
